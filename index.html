About

I am a finance professional with 4.5 years of experience spanning external audit, in-house accounting, and Group FP&A in Hong Kong. My investment work focuses on bottom-up fundamental analysis of US equities, supplemented by macro awareness, systematic valuation frameworks, and quantitative tools I build in Python.

I am joining the Rutgers Master of Financial Analysis program in September 2026, with a target graduation of May 2027 and a goal of transitioning into an equity analysis or investment role on the US East Coast. My writing on X documents my investment process, thesis construction, and market observations in real time.

My research spans five areas: China macro conditions, the Testing, Inspection and Certification industry, Technology and AI sector dynamics, reflections on the investing profession, and quantitative portfolio projects published on GitHub.
